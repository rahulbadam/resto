TV, film, festivals & more<\/h3>\n

Our crew catering<\/strong> service meets every need, whether on a TV or film set or backstage at a festival. We offer the best food at a reasonable price along with constantly updating menu options. No matter the location, our ingredients are fresh and our meals are packed with flavour. If a friendly and efficient crew caterer<\/strong> is what you are after, look no further.<\/p>\n

We endeavour to buy local and seasonal produce whenever we can and particularly love a wedge of Glastonbury Cheddar! Of course our ingredients are as fresh as possible and our coffee and tea always Fairtrade.<\/p>\n

Catering to every need<\/h3>\n

Always willing to adapt to various needs, we offer tons of veggie and vegan options. We don’t settle for the bog-standard alternatives, our menus are packed with creativity and are constantly changing.<\/p>\n

Red Radish go the extra mile for our planet – strictly using biodegradable plates, cups and other disposables! Why not read more about the Red Radish Sustainability policy<\/strong><\/a> and find out exactly what you should be eating and when!<\/p>\n

The best in the business<\/h3>\n

Last year we catered for 35 festivals across the UK, serving up around 50 tonnes of food in the process. In February, we were delighted to win the 2019 Festival Supplier Award for Best Crew and Artist Catering<\/strong>!<\/p>\n

Our journey on the festival circuit started back in 2012 when the lovely people at Glastonbury’s Shangri-La decided to give us a shot. Needless to say we’ve been back every year since, and now find ourselves running three field kitchens across the UK’s favourite festival – at Shangri-La, West Holts and The Common.<\/p>\n

Our list of festival partners hasn’t stopped growing since then. Glastonbury’s still close to our hearts at Red Radish but now one of many top UK festivals on our radar.<\/p>\n

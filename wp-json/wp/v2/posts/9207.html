Back in February, we were honoured to scoop the award for Best Crew and Artist Catering<\/strong> at the 2019 Festival Supplier Awards on behalf of our fantastic team!<\/p>\n

<\/p>\n

It was a pleasure to see the Red Radish name up in lights and a testament to the graft our team of Radishes put in all year round.<\/p>\n

In 2018 Red Radish catered for 35 festivals<\/strong> and a handful of feature films and TV shows. In the process we\u00a0served up around 50 tonnes<\/strong> of food to hundreds of happy crew and artists<\/strong>.<\/p>\n

We attended the awards night at London\u2019s Hurlingham Club this February excited to share stories with some friendly faces from the festival circuit. It was a delight to walk away with an award to boot!<\/p>\n

Of course it doesn\u2019t stop there\u2026 Our operation never stops growing and with the 2019 festival calendar very much in full-swing, we\u2019re already bigger and better.<\/p>\n

We\u2019ve upped our festival game this year, in addition to the usual favourites, we’ve taken on the likes of British Summer Time, Slam Dunk, Lost Village and Polo in the Park.<\/p>\n

Location catering<\/strong> is a big deal for us for 2019 and we\u2019re excited to be a part of some big projects in the world of TV and film.<\/p>\n
